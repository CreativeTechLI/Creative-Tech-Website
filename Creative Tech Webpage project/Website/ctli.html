What are we about?

Our club works to prepare prototypes for big named companies. For instance, we have used Dexter robots to collaborate with larger organizations sponsored by professionals. We have also showcased what we made around campus in several different NYIT events sponsored by the school. What we learned from this experience is that there are limitless opportunities in which we can evolve in our society as engineers.;
